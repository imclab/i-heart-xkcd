Welcome to i heart xkcd.

A simple XKCD comic viewer, with no ads!

i heart xkcd allows you to share comics with your friends, save comics for offline viewing and read the alt text on your iPhone or iPad. It also supports large comics and animated comics (sort of, see known issues below). Pure bliss!

Other features:

  • Single click a comic to view the alt text, favourite and share.
  • Double click to navigate. First, Last, Previous, Next, Random or to any of your Favourites.
  • Control what comic data is saved to your device in the settings.

Known issues:

  • Animated comics are a bit janky. I don't inspect the gif for the framerate yet, so it's a bit hit and miss.
  • The list of favourite comics doesn't expand to take up all the available height (yet).
